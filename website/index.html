Features

What it actually does

Short, accurate descriptions—mirroring how the background service worker and popup behave today.

◎
Active-tab time
Time accrues on a tracked site only while it is the selected tab in a focused Chrome window. Switch tabs, another app, or blur Chrome—counting pauses. You do not need to keep moving the mouse.
⏸
Break when the limit hits
The service worker starts a break when the currently focused tracked site’s own accumulated time reaches your interval (not “any combination” of sites). After the break, session usage resets; the overlay shows a live countdown, and you can allow an optional skip.
📊
Weekly social time chart
The popup chart is hours of social usage per weekday for this ISO week or last week—time on tracked sites while a break overlay is not showing. It is not a graph of break length or break count.
Pro — gated in extension
🎯
Tracked sites & break screens
Instagram, Facebook, and X/Twitter are included by default; you can add custom domains. Choose a break screen theme—calm default and cat animation are free; additional themes unlock with subscription in the extension.

The popup’s Weekly social time card matches what the extension records: active minutes on your tracked social sites, split by calendar day, while the break overlay is not on screen. Break time itself is not what the bars measure.
